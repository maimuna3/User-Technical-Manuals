GETTING STARTED


This is a User manual for the online recruitment system and has been created to give new users an insight into what the system entails and also to guide users on the processes of using the system. Below are instructions on how to go about achieving specific tasks on the system. The online recruitment system is designed to accommodate two kinds of users, the Administrator and Job Applicants. The administrator is allowed to Add new job vacancies, edit this jobs and delete them if need be. The Job applicants can then apply for the jobs added by the administrator. The applicants have to register on the system during their first application, once their account have been created, then they can apply. On subsequent visits to apply, the applicants only have to login then they are provided with a brief form which they have to fill and then upload their cover letter and resume. These processes are further explained below.

WEBSITE ADMINISTRATOR


The web administrator is the person incharge of the system and is the one that adds jobs, edit the jobs or delete them. This section provides information on how to perform the tasks of the administrator.


Adding Jobs


The following are steps to be taken in order to add a job to the system.

Step 1: Enter the login url "http://localhost:51178/login" into the address bar at the top of your browser

Step 2: Enter the Admin Username and Password which will be provided and click "Login". You will be redirected to the admin page

Step 3: On the admin header, click "Add Job" and you will be provided with a form.

Step 4: Fill the form provided with a job and its description and click "Add Job". see fig 5.0.



Editing Jobs



Follow the steps below to edit an existing job

Step 1: Login using the Admin Username and Password

Step 2: Click "Manage Jobs". see fig 6.1

Step 3: Select a job you wish to edit from the list of jobs and you will be redirected to an edit/delete page.

Step 4: Click "Edit this Job" and an edit job form will be provided. see fig 6.2

Step 5: Edit the form information and click "Update" to finalize the changes made.



Deleting Jobs



To delete a Job either because it's deadline has been reached, or for any other reason, follow the steps below

Step 1: Login using the Admin username and password

Step 2: Click "Manage Jobs"

Step 3: Select a job you wish to delete from the list of jobs and you will be redrected to the edit/delete page.

Step 4: Click the "Delete this Job" button and you will be asked to confirm tour delete. see fig 7.1

Step 5: Click the "Delete" button to delete the job see fig 7.2



JOB SEEKERS

Logging in


To apply for any job, you must be logged in, and to login you need to select a job you want to apply for.

Step 1: On the home page, click "Current Vacancies" to view the jobs currently available for application

Step 2: Select job of your choice

Step 3: Click "Apply for this Job" and a login form will be provided

step 4: Enter your login details and click login and you will be redirected to the application page. see fig 8.0



Registering


This is similar to the steps taken to log into the system. All you need to do is click the "New User" button instead of the login button (see fig 9.0) and you will be provided with the registration form. Fill this form and submit, fields with red asterix are required. Once an account has been created, you will be redirected to the application page. fill in that form and submit, then log out.
